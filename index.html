Research directions

The group focuses on atmospheric prediction, climate simulation, and the computing systems that make large experiments reliable.

Predictability and data assimilation

Linking observations, initial-condition uncertainty, and forecast error growth in complex atmospheric flows.

Climate process modeling

Examining physical mechanisms that shape extremes, circulation variability, and regional climate responses.

Scientific computing

Building transparent, repeatable workflows for model experiments, diagnostics, and large geophysical datasets.
